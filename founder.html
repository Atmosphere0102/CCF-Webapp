Read My Story

A Journey of Faith & Restoration

Hello, I am Ayodele Adeoye. For over [Number] years, I have been passionate about seeing marriages thrive. This passion wasn't born out of a perfect life, but through witnessing the transformative power of God's love in the midst of brokenness.

Like many, I saw families struggle with silence, unresolved conflict, and emotional distance. I realized that the church often taught us to stay married, but rarely taught us how to be happily married—especially when it came to the delicate topics of intimacy and deep emotional connection.

That gap in support birthed the Christian Couples Forum. My vision was to create a sanctuary—a safe, confidential haven—where couples could take off their masks and deal with real issues.

"Healing begins when we feel safe enough to speak the truth in love."

Today, I am honored to walk alongside couples as they navigate the beautiful, messy, and sacred journey of marriage. Whether you are newlyweds or celebrating your golden anniversary, my heart is to serve you with wisdom, grace, and unwavering faith.

Meet the Family

Behind every ministry is a family that believes in the vision. I am blessed to share this journey with my amazing spouse, Adeoye Esther, who has been my rock, prayer partner, and greatest encourager for 18 years.

Together, we have been gifted with wonderful children who constantly teach us about grace, patience, and unconditional love. Our home is our first ministry, and we strive to model the principles of CCF within our own walls—imperfectly, but authentically.
